About Me


I am a double degree student at Oberlin College and Conservatory studying Computer Science and Technology in Music and Related Arts. I'm current a teaching assistant for CS241 (System Programming) and I have previously been a teaching assistant for CS150 (Intro to CS) and CS151 (Data Structures). I finished my month-long internship at the Articulab at Cargenie Mellon University working on the Rapport-Aligning Peer Tutor project, and I'm working on writing my duo for double bass and electronics, and looking for summer research opportunities. I'm a member of the Oberlin College Computer Science Major Committee, and I'm very involved with Oberlin Programmers of Color (OPOC), and Oberlin ACM-W Chapter. I also sing in Collegium Musicum Oberlinese. When I'm not programming or composing, I'm probably crocheting or knitting at concerts or contra dancing.
